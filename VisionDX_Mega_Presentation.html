VisionDX Mega

AI-Powered Health Diagnostics

Pakistan Ka Digital Doctor

Platform Overview

VisionDX Mega is an advanced medical diagnostics demonstration platform.

  • Empowered by cutting-edge AI models (Groq Llama 3.2, Gemini).
  • Designed to provide comprehensive health assessments.
  • Supports multiple languages (e.g., Urdu, English).
  • Integrated with WhatsApp for widespread accessibility.

Core Mission

Democratizing healthcare through AI-driven insights.

  • AI Modules: 10+ specialized diagnostic engines.
  • Privacy-First: Local storage and secure API key management.
  • Accessibility: Reach patients where they are, online or offline via WhatsApp.

Comprehensive Diagnostic Suite

A unified platform for holistic health evaluation:

  • Cough Detector & Sleep Analyzer
  • Medicine & Lab Report Analyzer
  • Hair & Daily Routine Analyzer
  • Sign Language Translator
  • Offline Agent capabilities

Eye Predictor Module

Early detection of ocular conditions through image analysis.

  • Analyzes high-resolution eye images.
  • Identifies potential signs of cataracts, conjunctivitis, and other ailments.
  • Provides actionable insights and recommendations.

Skin Analyzer Module

Dermatological assessment powered by computer vision.

  • Detects rashes, lesions, and abnormal moles.
  • Classifies skin conditions with high accuracy.
  • Generates detailed reports for further medical review.

Wound Tracker Module

Continuous monitoring of wound healing progress.

  • Tracks wound size, color, and severity over time.
  • Alerts users to potential infections or stalled healing.
  • Creates a visual history for healthcare providers.

Technology Stack

Built with modern, scalable, and secure technologies:

  • AI Engines: Groq Llama 3.2, Google Gemini, Whisper AI

  • Integration: WhatsApp Business API
